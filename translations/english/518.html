Qaf

In the name of God, the Lord of Mercy, the Giver of Mercy!

1: Qaf By the glorious Quran!

2: But the disbelievers are amazed that a warner has come from among them and they say, ‘How strange!

3: To come back [to life] after we have died and become dust? That is too farfetched.’

4: We know very well what the earth takes away from them: We keep a comprehensive record.

5: But the disbelievers deny the truth when it comes to them; they are in a state of confusion.

6: Do they not see the sky above them––how We have built and adorned it, with no rifts in it;

7: how We spread out the earth and put solid mountains on it, and caused every kind of joyous plant to grow in it,

8: as a lesson and reminder for every servant who turns to God;

9: and how We send blessed water down from the sky and grow with it gardens, the harvest grain,

10: and tall palm trees laden with clusters of dates,

11: as a provision for everyone; how with water We give [new] life to a land that is dead? This is how the dead will emerge [from their graves].

12: The people of Noah disbelieved long before these disbelievers, as did the people of Rass, Thamud,

13: Ad, Pharaoh, Lot,

14: the Forest-Dwellers, Tubba: all of these people disbelieved their messengers, and so My warning was realized.

15: So were We incapable of the first creation? No indeed! Yet they doubt a second creation.
