56: On that Day control will belong to God: He will judge between them. Those who believe and do good deeds will be admitted to Gardens of Delight,

57: while those who disbelieve and reject Our revelations will receive a humiliating torment.

58: He will give a generous provision to those who migrated in God’s way and were killed or died. He is the Best Provider.

59: He will admit them to a place that will please them: God is all knowing and most forbearing.

60: So it will be. God will help those who retaliate against an aggressive act merely with its like and are then wronged again: God is pardoning and most forgiving.

61: So it will be, because God makes night pass into day, and day into night, and He is all hearing and all seeing.

62: So it will be, because it is God alone who is the Truth, and whatever else they invoke is sheer falsehood: it is God who is the Most High, the Most Great.

63: Have you [Prophet] not considered how God sends water down from the sky and the next morning the earth becomes green? God is truly most subtle, all aware;

64: everything in the heavens and earth belongs to Him; God alone is self-sufficient, worthy of all praise.
